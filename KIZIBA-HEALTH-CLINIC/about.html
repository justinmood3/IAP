🏥 Our Mission

Kiziba Health Clinic is a simple offline digital system designed to improve patient registration, tracking, and feedback collection in rural health centers.

💡 What This System Does

  • Registers patients offline
  • Stores patient data using localStorage
  • Allows patients to view their visit information
  • Collects feedback after consultation

⚙️ How It Works

1. Patient checks in using the form
2. Data is saved in the browser (offline storage)
3. Patient logs in using phone number
4. System shows visit details and feedback form

👨‍⚕️ Purpose

This system helps clinics reduce paperwork, improve patient tracking, and ensure better service delivery even without internet connection.
